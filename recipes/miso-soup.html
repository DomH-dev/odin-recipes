Miso Soup with Shiitake Mushrooms

Description:

A delicious Japanese soup with mushrooms and tofu.

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 20 mins

Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 litre vegetable broth
  • 4 shiitake mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 75g miso paste
  • 4 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 100g diced firm tofu
  • 2 green onions, trimmed and thinly sliced

Instructions

  1. Bring the vegetable broth to a boil in a saucepan. Add the mushrooms, reduce heat to low, and simmer 4 minutes.
  2. Stir the miso paste and soy sauce together in a small bowl.
  3. Add to the broth along with the tofu and continue cooking for 1 minute more.
  4. Pour the soup into bowls and top with the green onions to serve.

Nutritional info (per serving):

  • Calories: 92
  • Fat: 3g
  • Carbs: 12g
  • Protein: 6g
